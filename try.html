By setting a high z-index value like 999, you are ensuring that the navigation bar is positioned on top of other elements that may have a lower z-index value or no z-index specified. It's important to note that the z-index property only works on positioned elements (i.e., elements with a position value of something other than static). In the example I provided, the position: fixed; on the header element enables the use of z-index.
